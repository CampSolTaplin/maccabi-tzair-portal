# Madrichim & Mazkirut Portal Guide

Welcome to the Maccabi Tzair Miami portal! This guide walks you through
signing in for the first time and taking attendance for your group.

## 1. Open the portal

Go to:

**https://maccabi-tzair-dashboard-2-0.onrender.com**

You can open it from any device — phone, tablet, or laptop. It works in
any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox).

> Tip: bookmark it on your phone's home screen so you can reach it in one
> tap during Kinus.

  number. You can use any format — `(305) 555-1234`, `305-555-1234`, or
  just `3055551234`. The portal will recognize it automatically.

Click **Sign In**.

## 3. Set your own password

Because this is your first time, the portal will immediately send you to a

## 4. Your dashboard

When you log in, you land on the Madrich dashboard. The most important
thing you will see is a big coral-colored card labeled **Take Attendance**
with an arrow. This is the shortcut you'll use every week.

## 5. Taking attendance

### Step 1 — Open the attendance page

From your dashboard, tap the coral **Take Attendance** card.

### Step 2 — Pick a session

You will see two sections:

- **Open Sessions** — sessions that are still waiting for attendance.
  Today's session, if there is one, is highlighted with a coral ring and
  labeled **TODAY**.
- **Submitted Sessions** — past sessions where attendance has already
  been locked in. You can open these to review them but not to edit.

Tap the session you want to mark. In almost every case you'll tap the
one labeled **TODAY**.

### Step 3 — Mark each member

You'll now see the list of everyone in your group. For each person, pick
one of three status buttons on the right:

| Button | When to use it |
|---|---|
| **Present** (green) | The member came on time and is at the session. |
| **Late** (amber) | The member arrived, but later than the start time. |
| **Excused** (gray) | The member told you in advance they couldn't come, or had a legitimate reason (family event, illness, trip, etc.). |

At the top of the page there's a **progress bar** that shows how many
people you've marked out of the total (for example, `14/18`). You must
mark **every member** before you can submit.

If your group is big, you can use the **Search members...** box at the
top to jump to a specific name.

### Step 4 — Lock & submit

Once every member has a status, the coral **Lock & Submit Attendance**
button at the bottom will turn on.

Tap it.

- Your attendance for that session gets saved.
- The session becomes **locked** and read-only — you can still open it to
  view what you marked, but you can no longer change it.
- The session moves from "Open Sessions" to "Submitted Sessions".

If you realize you made a mistake **after** locking a session, contact an
administrator or coordinator. They can unlock it so you can fix it.

## 6. Signing out

Tap your name or avatar on the top-right corner and choose **Sign Out**.
Your session ends and you'll need your password again to come back.

## Common questions

**I don't see any sessions to mark.**
There are no open sessions for your group right now. This usually means
either the admin hasn't created sessions for the season yet or the next
session hasn't started. Contact your coordinator.

**I don't see any members in my group.**
Make sure you're assigned to the right group. If it looks empty, tell the
admin and they will check.

**I can't log in — I'm sure the password is right.**
If you tried several times with the wrong password, your account may be
temporarily locked. Wait a few minutes and try again. If the problem
continues, contact the admin to reset your password.

**I tapped a wrong status on a member and already hit "Lock & Submit".**
Ask an admin or coordinator to unlock the session. Once they unlock it,
open it again, fix the status, and hit "Lock & Submit" one more time.

**My phone died in the middle of marking — did I lose everything?**
No. Each time you tap a status, the portal saves it immediately. Pick up
where you left off when you get back in.
